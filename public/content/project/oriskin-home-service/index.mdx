## Overview

Oriskin Home Service lets customers use purchased non-medical treatments by booking an appointment at their own location, such as a home or another selected place.

## Business Context

The product extends Oriskin's clinic experience beyond branch visits. It helps customers who have already purchased eligible treatments schedule service without needing to come directly to a clinic.

## My Role

I worked as a backend engineer using Go. My focus was the backend workflow that supports customer scheduling, service availability, assignment, and operational state changes.

## Key Capabilities

- Appointment creation and scheduling
- Service availability by location
- Therapist assignment and dispatch workflow
- Order and appointment status management
- Integration with customer accounts and purchased treatment data
- Notification and reminder support
- Customer review flow for therapists
- Internal operations API support

## Technical Direction

The backend is built with Go and integrates with customer-facing and internal interfaces. The design centers on workflow state, availability checks, assignment coordination, and clear API boundaries for the applications around it.

## Outcome / Impact

Home Service helps Oriskin customers receive eligible non-medical treatment without visiting a clinic, extending customer convenience while still supporting internal operational coordination.
