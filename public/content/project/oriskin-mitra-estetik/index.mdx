## Overview

Oriskin Mitra Estetik is the therapist-side companion system for the Home Service ecosystem. It supports freelance aesthetic partners who handle customer appointments outside the clinic.

## Business Context

The concept is similar to a partner application inside a larger customer platform. Customers book services, while therapist partners need workflows for assignments, status updates, and service completion.

## My Role

I contributed on the backend side with Go, supporting workflows connected to appointment assignment and therapist operations.

## Key Capabilities

- Therapist assignment support
- Appointment acceptance and status workflow
- Partner-facing operational data
- Service completion flow
- Integration with customer Home Service appointments

## Technical Direction

The backend coordinates therapist-facing workflows with the customer-facing Home Service system. The implementation focuses on reliable state transitions and clear integration points between customer, operations, and therapist flows.

## Outcome / Impact

Mitra Estetik supports the operational side of Oriskin's home service model by giving therapist partners a structured workflow around assigned appointments.
