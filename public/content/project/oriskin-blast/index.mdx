## Overview

Oriskin Blast is an internal system for sending promotional broadcast messages to Oriskin customers. It supports controlled campaign workflows used by internal teams.

## Business Context

Promotional messaging needs to reach customers reliably while remaining manageable for internal operators. The system exists to make campaign delivery more structured than ad hoc manual processes.

## My Role

I worked on the backend side with Go and contributed to the production delivery of the system.

## Key Capabilities

- Broadcast campaign setup
- Customer targeting support
- Message delivery workflow
- Internal operator controls
- Production-oriented delivery flow

## Technical Direction

The system uses Go for backend services and Next.js for internal-facing workflows. The implementation focuses on predictable campaign execution, clear operational states, and maintainable service boundaries.

## Outcome / Impact

The system enabled internal teams to run customer promotion broadcasts through a dedicated tool. Sensitive delivery details and operational metrics are intentionally omitted.
